《The Matrix》（1999）

A groundbreaking sci-fi masterpiece blending philosophy, action, and stunning visuals. The battle between hackers and AI challenges perception, with the red and blue pills becoming iconic symbols of choice and truth.

《The Matrix Reloaded》（2003）

Dazzling action sequences and a more complex narrative expand the Matrix universe. The highway chase is legendary, while Neo’s journey as the chosen one delves deeper into philosophical themes.

《The Matrix Revolutions》（2003）

tThe epic conclusion intertwines war and faith. Neo’s ultimate sacrifice delivers a poignant message of hope and resolution. Though divisive, the finale leaves a lasting impression.
